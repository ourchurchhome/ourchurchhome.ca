Easter is almost here! We invite everyone to celebrate the resurrection of Jesus Christ with us across all three of our congregations.

**Good Friday** — April 3, 2026
A joint Good Friday service will be held at Morell United Church at 10:30 AM. All are welcome.

**Easter Sunday** — April 5, 2026
Each congregation will hold their regular Sunday service, beginning with Mount Stewart at 9:00 AM, Morell at 10:30 AM, and St. Peters Bay at 11:30 AM.

We look forward to worshipping together as a community of faith this Easter season.
